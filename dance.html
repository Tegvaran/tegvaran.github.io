Who is Teg?

Teg is a versatile artist hailing from Surrey, BC, who is training in styles like Hip-Hop and KRUMP. Representing the “illest illusionz” crew. For Teg, dance is a form of storytelling and emotional expression. He draws inspiration from the energy of the people around him, movie characters, and anime. His artistic approach involves the fusion of experimental concepts and the foundation of dance styles in unconventional ways, resulting in a distinctive and captivating movement.
